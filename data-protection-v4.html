Further information for website visitors according to Art. 13 GDPR

Purpose and legal basis of data processing (Art. 13 para. 1 c DS-GVO)

  • Handling and processing of contact requests (Art. 6 para. 1 f DS-GVO)*
  • Handling and processing of support requests (Art. 6 para. 1 b/f DS-GVO)*
  • Technical operation of the website (Art. 6 para. 1 f DS-GVO)*
  • Optimization of the website services by evaluating website usage data (Art. 6 para. 1 a/f DS-GVO)*
  • Enforcement of legal claims and defense in legal disputes
  • Ensuring IT security
  • Prevention of criminal acts
  • Actions for business management and further development of services and products
  • Ensuring the website functions

* Interests of the responsible party when weighing interests (Art. 13 para. 1 d DS-GVO)

Recipients or categories of recipients of the personal data (Art. 13 para. 1 e DS-GVO)

In the context of calling up and using services on our website, we pass on data to recipients such as plug-in producers, hosting providers, media and communication service providers. Important: We only pass on personal data to third party providers with your consent, to the extent this is required by data protection law.

Transfer to third countries (Art. 13 para. 1 f DS-GVO)

It is possible that, while calling up and using our website and associated services, data may be transmitted to third countries via subcontractors or companies associated with them. Potential risks may include unenforceable data subject rights and a lower level of data protection. We minimize the risk as far as possible by concluding order processing contracts (if such a contractual relationship exists) and standard contractual clauses including effective supplements required by the supervisory authorities.

Storage duration according to legal retention obligations (Art. 13 para. 2 a DS-GVO)

  • Web server (Hosting Provider): 8 weeks
  • Contact form: Your data will only be used to fulfill your request and will be deleted thereafter, as long as no legal, contractual or statutory retention obligation prevents it.
  • Cookies: Information on setting and deleting cookies can be found in the cookie instructions on our homepage.

Until the expiration of the storage period, the data transmitted to us will be protected by appropriate technical and organizational means to protect them from accidental or intentional manipulation, loss, destruction or access by unauthorized persons. Our security measures are continuously monitored and improved in accordance with technological developments and organizational possibilities.

Right of information, correction, deletion, restriction, data portability and objection (Art. 13 para. 2 b DS-GVO)

As a data subject, you have the right to information, correction and deletion of your data and to the restriction of processing, as well as a right to data portability at any time. For any of these purposes, please contact the responsible person using the contact details provided.

Right of objection (Art. 21 para. 1 DS-GVO)

Insofar as the processing of your data is carried out to protect legitimate interests, you have the right to object to this processing at any time by contacting us using the contact details provided, if reasons arise from your situation that conflict with this data processing. We will then terminate this processing unless it serves predominant interests worthy of protection on our part.

Right of withdrawal (Art. 13 para. 2 c DS-GVO)

If you have consented to the processing of your data, you have the right to revoke this consent at any time for the future. The lawfulness of the processing until the revocation is not affected by this. For this purpose, please contact the responsible office using the contact details provided.

Right of complaint (Art. 13 para. 2 d DS-GVO)

As an affected person, you can contact the responsible state commissioner for data protection and freedom of information in Baden-Württemberg at any time if you have a complaint.

Existence of a necessity to provide personal data (Art. 13 para. 2 e DS-GVO)

The collected data is necessary for the technical operation of the website, the processing of contact and appointment requests for newsletter subscription and support requests. A possible consequence of not providing your data may be that you cannot use offers on our website or not to their full extent.
