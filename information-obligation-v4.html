Storage period in accordance with statutory storage obligations (Art. 13 para. 2 a) GDPR)

As a rule, personal data is deleted within ten years after termination of the contractual relationship or even earlier if an interested party does not become a customer.

Right to information, correction, deletion, restriction, data portability and objection (Art. 13 para. 2 b) GDPR)

As a data subject, you have the right of access, correction and deletion of your data at any time and to restrictions on processing, as well as a right to data portability. Please contact the responsible person under the given contact data.

Right to object (Art. 21. para. 1 GDPR)

As far as the processing of your data takes place for the protection of justified interests, you have the right to object to this processing under our indicated contact data at any time, if reasons arise from your special situation, which oppose this data processing. We will then stop this processing unless it serves overriding interests worthy of protection on our part.

Right of withdrawal (Art. 13. para. 2 c) GDPR)

If you have consented to the processing of your data, you have the right to revoke this consent at any time for the future. The lawfulness of the processing until the revocation is not affected by this. To do so, please contact the responsible office using the contact details provided.

Right to lodge a complaint (Art. 13 para. 2 d) GDPR)

You can contact the State Commissioner for Data Protection and Freedom of Information Officer of Baden-Württemberg in the event of complaints at any time.

Requirement to provide personal data (Art. 13 para. 2 e GDPR)

The collected data is necessary for the processing of inquiries from interested parties, for the preparation of offers, the conclusion of purchase contracts or the execution of business operations.
