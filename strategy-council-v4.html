Most decisions never get
the perspectives they deserve.

Every serious management call should be looked at from several angles. Finance. Legal. HR. Product. Risk. In practice you rarely have those experts in the room at the same time, so decisions get made on a partial view and the blind spots surface later.

Why Achim Baier, CEO of arconsis IT Solutions, thinks every team should be using Strategy Council.

A council of experts,
composed for your question.

Strategy Council gives you a round of AI agents, each with a distinct role and skillset. A creative. A finance expert. An architect. Legal, HR, a visionary business developer. You pick the composition that fits the decision, hand them the problem, and let them think.

Pose the question.

You bring a real strategic decision. The kind you would normally carry into a meeting.

Compose the council.

Pick the expert roles the decision needs. Finance, legal, an architect, a visionary. Freely composed.

The swarm debates.

The agents run as an autonomous swarm. Each one argues its standpoint and pushes its perspective into the discussion.

Until they converge.

Round after round, every agent exchanges arguments with every other, until a consent score or a round limit ends it.

One clear recommendation.

A single condensed answer: the best-supported strategy, with the perspectives behind it. In minutes.

From open question
to validated direction.
